Juan José García Cárdenas

I am a PhD student at the Institute Polytechnique de Paris - École Nationale Supériore de techniques avancées - ENSTA, based in the Computer Sciences and Systems Engineering Laboratory (U2IS) working with professor Adriana Tapus. My research focuses on measuring how the collaborative tasks between humans and robots affect the cognitive load and trust of humans in robots. Also, the main idea of the project is to develop a machine-learning framework that allows robots to improve the cognitive load and trust of the operator. I'm part of the European RAICAM Project.
